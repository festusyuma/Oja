About OJA

OJA is an E-commerce company, offering a third-party online market platform for supermarkets and restaurants to provide their products and services to consumers.

OJA offers a solution to the widespread issue of poor accessibility to food and resources demanded by university students, primarily in Nigeria.

As a solution to this problem, OJA connects customers to supermarkets and restaurants in close proximity, via a third party app that provides an efficient ordering service at minimal cost to both parties involved (University students and Restaurants/Supermarkets).

The OJA app will be launched in Babcock University, a viable market that offers immense growth opportunities for both OJA and users of the app. After a successful launch and understudy of the market through monitoring feedback and responsiveness of the app, the E-commerce platform will be made available to other institutions nationwide, with tailored services offered to each institution depending on specific consumer demand from the market.
